Step by Step

How to Use Each Tool

Both tools are designed for zero learning curve. Follow these simple steps and have your bill estimate ready in under two minutes.

⚡
Meter-Based Calculator

1
Enter Consumer Details
Fill in consumer name, meter number, area/location, and select billing month. Bill number and date are auto-generated.
2
Input Meter Readings
Enter your previous reading and current reading in kWh. Units consumed is calculated automatically.
3
Set Charges & Currency
Add per-unit rate, fixed charge, optional other charges, and tax percentage. Select your local currency.
4
Preview, Download or Print
The bill preview updates live. Download your bill as a PDF or Excel file, or use the print button.
💡 Pro Tip

Check your electricity bill or utility meter card for the exact previous and current readings. The per-unit rate is usually printed on your monthly statement.

🔌
Appliance-Based Estimator

1
Enter Consumer & Rate Details
Fill in your name, location, and billing month. Set the per-unit rate, fixed charge, and currency.
2
Add Your Appliances
Click "Add Appliance" to add each device. Enter the appliance name, wattage (from the device label), quantity, daily hours of use, and number of days used.
3
Review Energy Breakdown
See per-appliance kWh calculation update live. The total units bar shows combined consumption.
4
Download Your Bill
Download a PDF with the full appliance table and charges breakdown, or export to Excel for further analysis.
💡 Pro Tip

You can find the wattage of any appliance on its back label, user manual, or product page. Start with the three pre-loaded sample appliances to see how it works.

Technical Information

Architecture

Both tools are single-file HTML applications. All logic, styling, and UI is contained within one .html file per tool. No build system, framework, or backend is required.

Technology Stack

  • HTML5 — Structure and markup
  • CSS3 — Custom properties, Grid, Flexbox, animations
  • Vanilla JavaScript — All calculations and DOM manipulation
  • jsPDF v2.5.1 — Client-side PDF generation
  • SheetJS (xlsx) v0.18.5 — Client-side Excel generation
  • Google Fonts — Typography (requires internet)

Calculation Logic

Meter-Based Tool: Units = Current − Previous. Energy Charge = Units × Rate. Subtotal = Energy + Fixed + Other. Tax = Subtotal × (Rate/100). Total = Subtotal + Tax.

Appliance Tool: Per appliance: kWh = (Watts/1000) × Hours/Day × Days × Quantity. Total kWh = sum of all appliances. Same billing formula applies after.

Browser Compatibility

Tested on Chrome 100+, Firefox 100+, Safari 15+, and Edge 100+. PDF and Excel download require modern browser APIs. Print functionality uses standard window.print().

Offline Usage

Core functionality works offline. Google Fonts and CDN libraries require internet on first load. If used from a CDN-cached version or saved locally with offline libraries, full offline usage is possible.

File Naming

Meter Tool PDF: electricity_bill_{meterNumber}_{month}.pdf | Appliance Tool PDF: electricity_bill_{billNumber}.pdf | Both Excel files follow the same naming pattern.
